Support the TAMU Speech and Debate Team

Your donations help us compete at tournaments, host events, and provide opportunities for students to enhance their public speaking and argumentation skills!

How to Donate

We appreciate any and all contributions. You can support us in the following ways:

  • Make a direct donation via our university's giving portal.
  • Sponsor a student or a team event.
  • Provide in-kind donations such as debate materials or travel sponsorships.

Donation Links

  • For donations less than $250, donations can be made on our Flywire Page.

  • To make an online donation at or above $250, please visit our University Giving Page to give through the Texas A&M Foundation.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about donating, please reach out to us at tamu.speechdebate@gmail.com.
